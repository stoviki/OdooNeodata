Quotation Templates

Select different templates for your quotations.

This module offers five different templates for your quotations that you can choose from. Preview our templates and select the one that you like the most. Some of the templates display the products sorted by their category.

Create Your Own Templates

You can create your own quotation templates depending on your needs.

Write the QWeb code for your template and you are done. There is no need for any additional settings.

Product Category Description

We have also added description and image for the Product Categories. You can print these fields in your quotations by choosing the corresponding template.

Partner Surveys

Upgrade the Survey module and survey your partners

Select Partner

Create a survey with the existing Survey module and link them to your partners. Ask your partners how content are they with the services you are providing, the products you are selling, the prices you are offering... You can survey each partner, whether it is a customer, a supplier or any type of partner in general. Just select the partner and fill the survey.

Preview Answers

Access the surveys directly from the partner's form and take a survey for the current partner. Also, you can preview all the surveys that the partner has taken and the answers that he/she has given.
